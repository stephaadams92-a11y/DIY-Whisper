DIY Whisper

Welcome

DIY Whisper is your calm, confidence‑building guide for everyday DIY tasks. Choose a trade, pick your task, and the app will tell you:

  • Whether you can safely do it
  • What you’ll need
  • How to do it step‑by‑step
  • Warnings, tips, and tricks
How to Use the App
  • Tap Menu to choose a trade
  • Select your task
  • Check the “Can I?” and “Needs” pages
  • Tap How To for clear instructions
  • Use Your Notes to save anything important
Important Safety Notice

This guide offers general DIY advice. Your safety and decisions are your responsibility. Only continue if you feel confident and capable.
